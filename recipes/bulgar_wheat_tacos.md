# bulgar wheat tacos

> source n/a

> serves 4

> 40 minutes

## Ingredients

- 1 cup bulgar wheat
- 2 cups stock (optional - can use water)
- cheese
- tomatoes
- burrito wraps
- taco seasoning
- peppers
- salsa
- sour cream
- onion

## Directions

cook the bulgar wheat in veggie stock or water.

fry with taco seasoning, optionally add chopped onions & peppers.

shread cheese, add to wrap & microwave to melt.

add bulgar & toppings.

consume.
